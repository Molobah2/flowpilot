FLOWPILOT — WHAT WE BUILT (plain English)
==========================================

THE SIMPLE VERSION
------------------
Imagine you run a small business and every time a client pays you,
you have to manually:
  - Send 50% to your co-founder
  - Send 35% to your contractor
  - Put 10% into a savings account you can't touch for 3 months
  - Keep 5% in your main account as a buffer

That's annoying. FlowPilot automates all of that — but for crypto.

You write the rules once ("Alice gets 50%, Bob gets 35%, reserve 10%,
hold 5%"). After that, every time money arrives, FlowPilot does the
splitting automatically, on the blockchain, no human needed.


WHAT'S ACTUALLY HAPPENING UNDER THE HOOD
-----------------------------------------
FlowVault is a smart contract (a self-executing program on the Stacks
blockchain) that can hold and route crypto tokens.

FlowPilot sits on top of it and does three things:

1. UNDERSTANDS your rules
   You write: "Alice 50%, Bob 35%, Reserve 10% locked until Aug 2026"
   FlowPilot converts that into exact instructions the blockchain accepts.

2. WATCHES your wallet 24/7
   A background program (the "runner") checks every 10 seconds if any
   money has arrived. When it does, it automatically runs the rules.

3. PROVES it worked
   Every transfer is a real blockchain transaction with a public ID.
   Anyone can look it up and verify it happened — no trust required.

We proved this works. During our test we sent 5 USDCx (test tokens)
to the treasury address while the runner was running unattended. It
detected the deposit automatically and split the funds correctly —
13 confirmed blockchain transactions as proof.


THE WEBSITE
-----------
The website is a window into your treasury. It shows you:
  - How much is in the vault right now
  - How much is locked (can't be touched until Aug 2026)
  - Every automation event that has happened
  - A tool to build new routing rules visually

Live site: https://flowpilot-production-1996.up.railway.app


HOW TO TEST THE SITE (step by step)
-------------------------------------

STEP 1 — Open the dashboard
Go to: https://flowpilot-production-1996.up.railway.app
You should see a dark dashboard load automatically with real numbers.

STEP 2 — Check the Overview page (you're already on it)
Look for these 4 cards at the top:
  - Treasury Balance  →  should show ~0.7500 USDCx
  - Locked Reserve   →  should show ~0.5000 USDCx (locked until Aug 2026)
  - Automated Events →  should show a number (our test runs)
  - Success Rate     →  should show 100%

Below that you'll see:
  - A flow diagram showing how money moves (Incoming → Engine → Alice/Bob/Reserve/Hold)
  - AI Insights panel with smart observations about your treasury
  - A live activity feed of every blockchain transaction

STEP 3 — Click "Automate" in the left sidebar
This is the policy builder. It's a 4-step wizard:
  Step 1: Choose when automation runs (we use "On Every Deposit")
  Step 2: Add who gets paid and what percentage
  Step 3: Set how much to lock up and for how long
  Step 4: Review the full summary

Notice the Live Preview panel on the right updates as you change things.
No JSON or blockchain code is ever shown to the user — it stays hidden.

STEP 4 — Click "Vault" in the left sidebar
This shows the raw blockchain state of the FlowVault contract:
  - Total, unlocked, and locked balances
  - How many blocks until the lock expires
  - The active routing slot

STEP 5 — Click any transaction in the activity feed (Overview page)
It opens the Hiro Explorer (blockchain explorer) showing the actual
on-chain record of that transaction — proof it really happened.
